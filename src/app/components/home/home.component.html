About BCen

If plan A[cen] fails, plan BCen! BCen is more than just a mini-microcon to fill the void. Enter a blissful world away from the panaderia where pop culture, cosplayers, and fantastic nostalgia collide.


Feel like a kidult again in a place free of judgement and overflowing with creative expression, endless fun, and good spirits. Founded in 2020 by Brianna, brought to life by congoers like you. We hope you enjoy a diverse, equitable, inclusive, welcoming first annual BCen.

Rules

  1. Self Check-in: After 3:00 PM / Checkout: 11:00 AM
  2. No smoking
  3. No pets
  4. No parties or events
  5. Be a clean, responsible, respectable kidult!
  6. Have fun!
