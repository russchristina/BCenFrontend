About BCen

A blissful world away from the Panaderia. Where cosplayers and fans of many walks can relive the nostalgic memories and joy of feeling like a kidult again amidst hours of friendship and fantasy at a con. A place free of judgement and overflowing with love for each other's creativity and expression of favorites in the realm of pop culture and entertainment. A place where fandoms collide. BCen a mini-con conceived in 2020 by founder Brianna as the intersection of all of these things. Diverse, Equitable, Inclusive.
